About Us

Who Are We?

Established in 1988, Florist is a second-generation family run business bringing you more than 29 years of experience and expertise in floral arrangements.

We began our journey as a small retailer of fresh-cut flowers and simple bouquets. Today with the growth and development of our company, we have added a variety of services and products to our company.
