Return/Exchange/Cancelation Policy

Unfortunately, we do not offer refunds. But if you are not completely satisfied with your flowers we will happily replace your Florist purchase or give you store credit.

Get in touch with us if you have any issues
  1. If an order is filled and delivered to the recipient and there is a question of the quality of the arrangement, the recipient should contact Florist directly. In almost every case, we will gladly replace the arrangement. To request a replacement, Florist must be contacted within 24 hours of the delivery. Bear in mind that flowers are perishable. Proper care is required. No refunds or replacements will be made for any order should the recipient or sender not notify us within 1 day of the original delivery date. It is the responsibility of the sender to review all of the information provided on this website relating to refunds and/or replacements.
  2. Should any customer place an order through Florist and then need to cancel the order, the cancellation must be made prior to the delivery of the order. If the cancellation is received after the order is delivered, no refund can be issued.
